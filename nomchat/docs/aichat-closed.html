⚠️ Service Notice

AI Chat Company — Service Closed

AI Chat Company has stopped supporting the Nomchat service. The service has been shut down due to technical issues.

What happened

AI Chat Company, which previously integrated with Nomchat ID for authentication, has permanently closed its service. Users who signed in via Nomchat ID will no longer be able to access the platform.

Impact

  • All AI Chat Company accounts are no longer accessible
  • Nomchat ID tokens issued for AI Chat Company are now invalid
  • Chat history and user data are no longer available

What you can do

Your Nomchat ID account remains active and unaffected. You can continue using Nomchat ID with other connected services.

Note: This closure does not affect your Nomchat ID account or any other services connected to it.
