AI Chat Company — Service Closed

AI Chat Company has stopped supporting the Nomchat service. The service has been shut down due to technical issues.

⚠️ Service Unavailable

AI Chat Company is no longer operational. All connections to this service have been terminated.

What happened

AI Chat Company, which previously integrated with Nomchat ID for authentication, has permanently closed its service due to technical issues. Users who signed in via Nomchat ID will no longer be able to access the platform.

Impact on users

  • All AI Chat Company accounts are no longer accessible
  • Nomchat ID tokens issued for AI Chat Company are now invalid
  • Chat history and user data are no longer available

Your Nomchat ID is safe

This closure does not affect your Nomchat ID account or any other services connected to it. Your account remains active and unaffected.

No action required

You can continue using Nomchat ID with other connected services normally.
